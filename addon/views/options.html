Check your master password

Lookup in leaked/breached passwords databases (provided by Have I Been Pwned) for the presence of your master password. This service is also used by Mozilla's Firefox Monitor.

Do not worry, your password is NOT transmitted, only the first 5 characters of the hashed version are used to perform this check. This follows the implementation of the k-Anonimity model.
